About Us

Mint Education Services Limited is a modern educational services and outsourcing company created in response to the post-pandemic shift in Nigeria’s learning landscape. We provide professional educators, teaching assistants, tutors, early childhood workers, and comprehensive academic support services to schools, families, teachers, students, and institutions.
